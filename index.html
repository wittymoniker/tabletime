The mission is to create social media exchange and localized life organization in a one-to-one manner which prioritizes a 50-50 life management and realism balance based on a decentralized host network. Because the network mechanics work to decentralize and minimize the need for enlarging data centers. In the network you will be able to more quickly and effectively search for users posess a higher number of active mutual friends, relevant users that are a higher measurement popularity, popular groups based on interest and distance by network linkage, and popular events based on location and similarity as well.
